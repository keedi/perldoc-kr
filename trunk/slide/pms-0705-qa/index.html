따옴표 안에서 값 출력하기!

별로~

그런데 왜?

100번에 1번 정도는 유용할 때가...

print "30 + 50 = ", 30 + 50, "\n";

---
30 + 50 = 80


print "30 + 50 = 30 + 50\n";

---
30 + 50 = 30 + 50


print "30 + 50 = ${30 + 50}\n";

---
Can't use string ("80") as a SCALAR ref
while "strict refs" in use at test.pl line 24.


print "30 + 50 = ${ \(30 + 50) }\n";

---
30 + 50 = 80

:-)

We got the POWER!!

my $num1 = 30;
my $num2 = 50;
print "$num1 + $num2 = ", $num1 + $num2, "\n";

---
30 + 50 = 80

이제!

my $num1 = 30;
my $num2 = 50;
print "$num1 + $num2 = ${ \( $num1 + $num2 ) }\n";

---
30 + 50 = 80

CooL~!
